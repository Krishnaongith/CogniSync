# What's New in CogniSync 2.0

## 🎨 Complete Visual Redesign

Your learning platform just got a major upgrade! We've transformed CogniSync with modern design patterns, engaging animations, and powerful new features.

## ✨ New Features

### 📊 Progress Tracking Dashboard
Track your learning journey with:
- **Documents Processed**: See how many documents you've simplified
- **Reading Time**: Total time spent learning
- **Streaks**: Daily visit tracking with fire emoji 🔥
- **Achievements**: Unlock badges as you progress

### 🏆 Achievement System
Earn badges for milestones:
- 🎯 **First Steps** - Process your first document
- 📚 **Bookworm** - Reach 5 documents
- 🏆 **Scholar** - Hit 10 documents
- 🔥 **On Fire** - Maintain a 3-day streak
- ⏰ **Time Master** - Accumulate 60+ minutes of reading

### 📈 Quick Stats Cards
Get instant insights:
- Key points extracted
- Task completion progress with visual bar
- Estimated reading time

### 🎯 Floating Action Button
Quick access to:
- Upload new documents
- Export your content (coming soon)
- Share with others (coming soon)

### 🎭 Collapsible Sections
Organize your workspace by collapsing/expanding card sections

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Start the backend: `cd server && node index.js`
2. Start the frontend: `cd cogni-sync && npm run dev`
3. Upload a document and watch the magic happen!
